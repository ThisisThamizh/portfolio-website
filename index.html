About me

My name is Thamizhventhan Kannadas, and I am from Jaffna, Sri Lanka. I started my journey

My main goal as a web developer is to continuously educate myself on the latest

Whether you require a basic website, an e-commerce platform, or a sophisticated web
application, I possess the technical proficiency and expertise to deliver high-quality
websites that are quick to respond, easily accessible, and user-friendly for everyone.
